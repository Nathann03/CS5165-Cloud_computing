Retail Question 1

Which household and product factors are shaping retail engagement?

This dashboard answers the class retail questions by showing how demographics, spend trends, category demand, brand mix, basket pairs, and seasonality change across the dataset.

  • Demographics and engagement: income, loyalty, household mix, and region
  • Engagement over time: monthly spend and items per basket
  • Basket analysis: common commodity pairs and cross-sell patterns
  • Brand preferences: national vs. private brand mix and organic behavior
  • Churn analysis: a ranked list of households most at risk of disengaging

What To Look For

How to read this dashboard

  1. The KPI cards summarize overall household reach, transaction volume, and average basket behavior.
  2. The time-series and category charts highlight where spending and product demand are rising or falling.
  3. The basket pairs section shows which commodity combinations appear together most often.
  4. The churn table identifies households with the strongest signs of disengagement risk.
